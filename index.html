How to Add Personal Ventures to LinkedIn

Highlight your personal projects and achievements by adding them to your LinkedIn profile.

  1. Log in to your LinkedIn account and go to your profile.
  2. Under the "Experience" section, click the "+ Add position" button.
  3. Enter the following details:
    • Company Name: Personal Ventures
    • Position: Your role (e.g., Creator, Innovator)
    • Location: (Optional)
    • Employment Type: Self-Employed
    • Start Date: The date you began your personal ventures
    • End Date: (Optional) If the project has a specific end date
    • Description: Describe your personal ventures and their significance.
  4. Click the "Save" button to add this position to your profile.
  5. Now, your personal projects are prominently displayed on your LinkedIn profile, allowing others to learn about your achievements and contributions.
